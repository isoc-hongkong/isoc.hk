### Tracking Technologies: IP addresses, Domains and Cookies

We do automatically receive and record information from the computer and browser of people visit this Site, including your IP address, domain, and the pages you request for traffic analysis and generation of aggregated statistics reports. In the case of any unlawful or abuse of use of our web site, ISOC HK may disclose the IP addresses and domains record.

Some of this information may be gathered through the use of "cookies". A cookie is an element of data that a website can send to your browser, which may then store it on your system. Cookies do not read data off your hard disk and can only contain personal information that you provide yourself. If we use cookies to collect your personal data, you will be duly informed. You may disable the cookies by changing your browser setting. Refusing cookies may not allow for the full functionality or use of our web site.
