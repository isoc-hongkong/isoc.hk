## ISOC HK Membership - Individual Members

ISOC HK is driven by individual users and Internet professionals, in conjunction with and augmented by socially responsible corporations in the information society. ISOC HK intends to encourage participation and discussion from its members on issues within the scope of its mandate, and endeavours to partner with other relevant groups from different spectrums of the local and global community on different activities……

#### General Member

Any person over 18 years old, living or working in Hong Kong SAR, and having an email address is eligible to apply for General Membership.

General Members are those individual members who are NOT required to pay the membership fees. General Members are voting members whose right to vote is limited to the election of the Executive Committee. General Members are entitled to hold office and attend any meetings of the Association which they are entitled to attend.

Please complete the [online membership application form](https://portal.internetsociety.org/622619/form/join "Link to Membership Application") of ISOC International here, then click "Join a chapter" and search for Hong Kong.

#### Full Member

Any person over 18 years old, living or working in Hong Kong SAR, and having an email address is eligible to apply for Full Membership.

The membership fee for Full Member is $200/ year.

Full Members are those individual members who are required to and have fully paid up the membership fee. Full Members are voting members who shall have the right to vote in all matters in the General Meetings. Full Members shall have the right to elect the Executive Committee, to hold office, and attend and vote at any meetings of the Association which they are entitled to attend.

Please complete the [online membership application form](https://portal.internetsociety.org/622619/form/join "Link to Membership Application") of ISOC International here, then click "Join a chapter" and search for Hong Kong.

#### Payment for FULL MEMBER

If you apply to be our FULL MEMBER (Membership fee: $200/year), please make your membership fee payment after completing the online application form. Below are the payment methods:

##### 1) Online credit card payment

Please go to [https://www.isoc.hk/full-memberships/](/full-memberships/) and you will be directed to the payment page. After successfully making the payment, please inform us at [sec@isoc.hk](mailto:sec@isoc.hk).

##### 2) Online payment by bank transfer

Please transfer the money to 228-267696-001 (Hang Seng Bank Limited) and save the online transfer receipt. Then email the transfer receipt to [sec@isoc.hk](mailto:sec@isoc.hk). Please write down your name, email address and mobile phone no. in your email with SUBJECT: _Online Payment of Membership Fee – [Your Name]_.

##### 3) Direct bank-in

Please bank-in the money to 228-267696-001 (Hang Seng Bank Limited) and pencil your name, email address and mobile phone no. at the back of the ORIGINAL bank-in slip. Then mail it to Internet Society Hong Kong, 12/F, Daily House, 35-37 Haiphong Road, Tsim Sha Tsui, Hong Kong.

##### 4) Payment made by cheque

Please make a crossed cheque, payable to "**Internet Society Hong Kong Limited "** and pencil your name, email address and mobile phone no. at the back. Then mail the cheque to Internet Society Hong Kong, 12/F, Daily House, 35-37 Haiphong Road, Tsim Sha Tsui, Hong Kong.

**Note:** ISOC HK shall have the absolute discretion in approving any application for membership. Membership shall commence on acceptance by ISOC HK in writing and on payment by the applicant of the required fees. All membership fees once paid shall not be refundable.
