**現正接受提名及報名** **Call For Nominations & Entries** 「以創新意念助城市設計實踐以人為本」 “Innovation for Walking” | 由**香港互聯網協會及[思匯政策研究所](http://civic-exchange.org) **聯合主辦的 Walk21HK CityTech Awards，旨在嘉許改善「步行」體驗或鼓勵步行習慣的創新科技產品及構思 — 當中包括，但不限於任何科技應用，如道路裝置、手機應用程式、配件、穿戴裝置等等。 Co-organized by **Internet Society Hong Kong (ISOC HK)** &**[Civic Exchange](http://civic-exchange.org/)**, 'The Walk21HK CityTech Awards' is to celebrate innovations for walking, how the integration of “smart” elements into urban settings has changed the modern walking experience******– from the integration of street facilities, apps on phones, tablets and wearable gadgets to technologies that encourage new walking habits and anything in between.**
