|    |  ![](https://ci3.googleusercontent.com/proxy/Np8zgDR5vnRAkM2wmJGpIyZ-joG6u8UqjC85hRcw-wsVN5qNkQtUP8PRSJHZvUhh9PMOZlGOblLrKyZgCAEb8BPH0jrhJLU=s0-d-e1-ft#https://www.hkirc.hk/eDM/eDM19/images/nothing.png) |    |  |  To cope with the ever-changing digital world, HKIRC is organising the 6th Digital Marketplace Seminar (DMP), themed "Would O2O open doors for local business". The DMP Seminar provides a sharing platform for local businesses, SMEs, industry pioneers and participants to exchange insights and get first-hand market knowledge. This year, the focus will be on how businesses can integrate e-commerce with its traditional business, making a strategic move to build a more convenient shopping experience and promote brands through the use of technology. Talks, exhibitions and forums will be organised at DMP. It is a great opportunity for participants to get ahead of the crowd and explore opportunities in the era of O2O, [REGISTER NOW!](http://enews.cyberport.hk/track?t=c&mid=1281082&uid=22636713&&&https%3A%2F%2Fwww.hkirc.hk%2Fdmp%2F2014%2Fregistration_eng.html) 由HKIRC舉辦數碼市場研討會(DMP)已是第六年舉行。隨著互聯網市場瞬間萬變，今年DMP的主題是  
「2014數碼市場－O2O為香港企業開展營商新陣勢?」。在研討會上，本地公司、中小企、業界領先和與會者將探索電子商貿及傳統業務的整合發展，並運用策略及科技推廣品牌，務求令客戶享有最便捷的購物體驗。與會者更可透過DMP內的研討會、展覽、論壇掌握市場第一手資訊，在O2O發展機遇中捷足先登，[請即登記!](http://enews.cyberport.hk/track?t=c&mid=1281082&uid=22636713&&&https%3A%2F%2Fwww.hkirc.hk%2Fdmp%2F2014%2Fregistration_b5.html)
