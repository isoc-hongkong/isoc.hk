**" Building Trust in Cloud Computing" Summit 2012 and Inauguration of CSA Hong Kong & Macau Chapter**  
Hosted by Internet Society Hong Kong and Cloud Security Alliance - Hong Kong & Macau, join us for the one day summit to learn more about Security Assurance with Cloud Computing. It will possibly be one of the most prominent cloud security events in Southern China. Don't miss the chance to celebrate the formation of CSA Hong Kong & Macau Chapter with us!**Thanks to ISOC 's support for global video streaming, the first eight videos on this Youtube channel is from this event:** http://www.youtube.com/user/ISOCchapters/videos   Photos   **Speaker 's Presentations** Andy Thurai (Intel) - [Andy_Intel.pdf](http://archive.isoc.hk/Andy_Intel.pdf) Jim Reavis (Cloud Security Alliance) - [Jim_CSA-global-efforts-stock-preso.pdf](http://archive.isoc.hk/Jim_CSA-global-efforts-stock-preso.pdf) Michael Yung (Next Media Group) - [Michael_NextMedia_ApplicationOfCloudTechnologyInMediaIndustry.pdf](http://archive.isoc.hk/Michael_NextMedia_ApplicationOfCloudTechnologyInMediaIndustry.pdf) Henry Chang (Office of the Privacy Commissioner for Personal Data, Hong Kong) - [Privay Compliance - The Road to Trusted Platform.pdf](http://archive.isoc.hk/Privay%20Compliance%20-%20The%20Road%20to%20Trusted%20Platform.pdf) Maverick Tam (Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu) - [Deloitte CSA Summit -20120517.pdf](http://archive.isoc.hk/Deloitte%20CSA%20Summit%20-20120517.pdf) Tim Grance (National Institute of Standards and Technology) - [Tim-NIST-ObservationsOnCloudComputing.pdf](http://archive.isoc.hk/Tim-NIST-ObservationsOnCloudComputing.pdf)
