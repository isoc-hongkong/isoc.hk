**" IPv6 in Action" Project for SMEs and General Public, sponsored by Hong Kong SAR Government **  
| ![Logo - IPv6](/images/uploads/2013/01/Image-IPv6-logo.png) | Internet Society Hong Kong (ISOC-HK) is proud to announce that it has been awarded by the Office of the Government Chief Information Officer (OGCIO) to lead the "IPv6 in Action" project. The project's aim is to conduct consumer education and awareness program for IPv6 services in Hong Kong. ISOC-HK will target the general public and Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) to deliver messages related to IPv4 exhaustion, IPv6 requirements and opportunities.

**IEEE Distinguished Lecturer talk - Storing Your Life - Your Part of the Zetabyte Future  
Thomas M. Coughlin, President, Coughlin Associates  
**December 9, 2011 (5:30pm) @ City University of Hong Kong This presentation discusses the drivers for consumer digital storage, the different mobile and static usage models for digital storage in consumer devices and the resulting consumer storage hierarchy. Important characteristics of consumer storage devices are shown and guidelines are given for how digital storage should be designed in consumer devices.
