ISOC HK is supporting the following events:

  * Information Security Summit
  * Build a Secure Cyberspace Seminar
  * Cybersecurity Consortium

![](/images/uploads/2018/08/Information-Security-Summitbackground.png)

The Information Security Summit 2018 is a regional event with the aim to give participants from the Asia Pacific region an update on the latest development, trends and status in information security. Overseas and Local Experts from the industry will be invited to share their experience and knowledge.

**Date:** 4-5 September 2018

**Venue:** Level 4, Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre, Wanchai, Hong Kong

More Information and Registration: <https://www.issummit.org/>

![](/images/uploads/2018/08/Screenshot-2018-08-28-at-11.49.32-AM.png)

The seminar will invite information security experts to share their experiences on cyber threats. The topic will focus on raising awareness of phishing scams within the organization, strengthening the network security of the organization, improving the security of the organization's website, and mobile security issues.

**Date:** 20 September 2018  
**Time:** AM Session: 9:30am - 12:45am; PM Session: 2:30pm - 5:30pm  
**Venue:** Conference Hall, 4/F, HKPC Building, 78 Tat Chee Avenue, Kowloon Tong, Kowloon

More Information and Registration: <https://www.hkcert.org/my_url/en/event/18092001>

![](/images/uploads/2018/08/banner_v1.jpg)

 

 

 

The Consortium aims at bringing together international cyber security experts, senior management in public and private sectors, IT practitioners and LEAs to exchange knowledge and intelligence on cyber attacks and share best practices on critical infrastructures protection and incident response.

**Date:** 23-24 October 2018 (Main Conference); and 25 October 2018 (Half-day session for Law Enforcement Agencies)  
**Venue:** Police Headquarters, No. 1 Arsenal Street, Wan Chai, Hong Kong

More Information and Registration: <https://cybersecurityconsortium.net/>
