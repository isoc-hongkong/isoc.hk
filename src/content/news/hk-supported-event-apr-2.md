Regional Policy Survey 2017 by  
Internet Society Asia-Pacific Bureau****** An international survey aims to gain further insights on your participation in government policymaking for the Internet, as well as your views on policies related to Trust and Data Protection.  - It is important for us to have as wide range of input as possible from multiple sectors in the survey. This will help better capture the sentiments, needs and concerns of Internet users and stakeholder groups in the region.  - The findings of the survey will provide valuable input to our engagement with regional and local policymaking bodies, and ensure that our priorities in the region reflect those of our community. - The survey report will also be used as input in multiple fora in the region to better inform dialogue and deliberations in and around Internet issues.
