The Internet Society Hong Kong has supported the  
Government's initiative for implementing Electronic Health Record Sharing  
System since it was proposed. We believe  
this initiative will be beneficial for the better quality of healthcare  
provision, as well as fostering the development of Hong Kong as a hub of  
healthcare IT, both in terms of technology application and knowledge.   [ISOC-HK-eHealth_response.pdf](https://www.isoc.hk/ISOC-HK-eHealth_response.pdf)
