[![HKICT_web banner](/images/uploads/2014/11/HKICT2015_web-banner-0111.jpg)](/images/uploads/2014/11/HKICT2015_web-banner-0111.jpg) Hong Kong ICT Awards 2015: Best ICT Startup Award – Launch Party and Sharing Session Have you just started your own company? Ever wondered what the secrets to success are? Best ICT Startup Award is the 2nd year organized by Internet Society Hong Kong. It is to recognize ICT startups that have developed innovative and quality ICT solutions, promote entrepreneurship and startup culture, foster the startup community and its whole ecosystem in Hong Kong, encourage companies to apply ICT to create social impact for the benefit of the society. The application for Best ICT Startup Award is now opened till 16 Jan 2015, noon. At the launch party on 8 Dec night, besides announcing the details of the Award, our keynote speaker, Mr. Nicholas Yang, former CEO of Cyberport will share how he managed to turn Cyberport’s operation cash positive and delivered an impressive cumulative surplus. Our startups friends from Teamnote and Shopline will share how they get into the top-tier accelerator programs in Silicon Valley and their startup stories.
