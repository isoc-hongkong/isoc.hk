**World IPv6 Launch x IPv6 in Action x IPv6World:Asia**  
**_Is Asia Pacific and China doing well on IPv6 Deployment?_**  
Organized by the Internet Society, and building on the successful one-day World IPv6 Day event held on 8 June 2011, World IPv6 Launch represents a major milestone in the global deployment of IPv6. As the successor to the current Internet Protocol, IPv4, IPv6 is critical to the Internet's continued growth as a platform for innovation and economic development. Speakers from Asia Pacific and China will share their real experience on World IPv6 Day last year and deployment case studies. Don't miss it! **Date:** June 6, 2012 (Wed) **Time:** 2:00pm - 5:30pm **Venue:** Ocean View Court, The Arcade, Cyberport, 100 Cyberport Road, Hong Kong **Fee:** Free **Register:** [Click here](http://ipv6now.hk/en/Event_WhatsNew_Details.php?id=4) **Media Release:** [ISOC HK_IPV6launch_06JUN12_Eng_finalfinal](/images/uploads/2012/04/ISOC-HK_IPV6launch_06JUN12_Eng_finalfinal.pdf)   **新聞稿：** [ISOC HK_IPV6launch_06JUN12_Chi_final](/images/uploads/2012/04/ISOC-HK_IPV6launch_06JUN12_Chi_final.pdf) **IPv6 in Action Consumer Guide Launch Press Release Video:**   **Photos:
