The assessors and judges of the Award, incubators, investors, business partners and startup friends are all welcome to join! Please register now!  
  
  
Date: 10 Apr, 2015 (Fri)  
Time:  
12:00pm – 12:30pm Ceremony,N201 HKCEC  
12.45pm - 2.30pm Lunch Gathering, restaurant near HKCEC (tbc)  
Language: English and Cantonese  
  
Entry Fee:   
Ceremony: Free of Charge  
Lunch: $250/ head. (Discounted price of $200 for ISOC HK members/ Partners/ Supporting Organisations)
