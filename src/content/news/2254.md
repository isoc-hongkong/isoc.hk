|  **"Internet of Things - From Vision to Reality" Conference **  
---  
What will happen when things, homes and cities become smart? It will unleash a wave of new possibilities for data gathering, predictive analyses, and IT automation. This is “**Internet of Things** ” (IoT). Internet Society Hong Kong has been promoting IPv6 in Hong Kong in the past few years. Moving forward, one of the next new trends on Internet technology is "Internet of Things". The upcoming conference is jointly organized by **ISOC HK** and **IET HK**. It will cover both research and real case study. We will bring you from vision to reality. Are you ready?
