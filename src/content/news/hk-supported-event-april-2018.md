“ICT Conference 2018 – Intelligence connecting your future”, aims to provide a forum for the government officials, university researchers, and major industry professionals to share the views on the government policy, the latest development and applications, as well as the opportunities and threats in the future ICT era. 

Rapid development of digital technologies causing the changes in our daily life like the big data operations such as capture, curation, storage, search, sharing, transfer, analysis, and visualisation, etc., Internet of Things (IoT) which offered advanced connectivity of devices, systems, and services that goes beyond machine-to-machine communications (M2M) and covers a variety of protocols, domains, and applications. These digital technologies can enhance the modern life while the challenges on security and privacy are also increasing remarkably. In addition, the advancement of digital technologies also provides unprecedented business opportunities for the various industries particularly in the local ICT industry.

  
This conference is an excellent platform for us to exchange views on the latest technology development of Information Technology and associated applications among the professionals for local IT industries that deriving better life in near future.

The objectives of this Conference are share the views on:

  * Latest ICT developments
  * The ICT policy in HK
  * How ICT benefits our future livings
  * How ICT threats can be mitigated
  * Potential Business Opportunity
