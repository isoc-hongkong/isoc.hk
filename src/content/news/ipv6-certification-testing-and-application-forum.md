HOW TO GET AN IPV6 READY LOGO? - IPv6 Certification Testing and Application Sharing  
Today our daily life has been attached very closely with the internet and we are seeing the tremendous growth in the internet traffics. In fulfilling our future needs, new technologies and protocols have been developed to assist us in meeting these needs. The IPv6 technology has been developed to help us in advancing our internet services to the new era and in resolving the challenges in the new service offerings. Therefore, the test support of IPv6 technology will be in much greater needs in order to cope with the IPv6 network deployment and surely will see how the IPv6 technology will benefit to our new daily life in the near future. Co-hosted by Internet Society Hong Kong (ISOC HK) and Hong Kong Science & Technology Parks Corporation (HKSTPC), the IPv6 Certification Testing and Application Sharing Forum is going to bring the audience for knowledge sharing on the development of IPv6 and the available testing. In collaboration with BII (China) – a IPv6 Forum approved test lab, HKSTPC is introducing the IPv6 Ready Logo Programme and certification testing to the local market. | **Forum Details:**
