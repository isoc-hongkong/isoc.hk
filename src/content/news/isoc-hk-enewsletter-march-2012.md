**World IPv6 Launch - The Future is Forever! ** ![Image - world IPv6 Launch](/images/uploads/2013/01/Banner-logo2.png) |  Major Internet service providers (ISPs), home networking equipment manufacturers, and web companies around the world are coming together to permanently enable IPv6 for their products and services by **6 June 2012**.Organized by the Internet Society, and building on the successful one-day World IPv6 Day event held on 8 June 2011, World IPv6 Launch represents a major milestone in the global deployment of IPv6. As the successor to the current Internet Protocol, IPv4, IPv6 is critical to the Internet's continued growth as a platform for innovation and economic development.[World IPv6 Launch Website](http://www.worldipv6launch.com/) [Find out more about what happened on World IPv6 Day (Jun 8, 11) and what will happen on World IPv6 Launch.](http://www.apricot2012.net/__data/assets/pdf_file/0019/45172/gaurab-world-ipv6-launch.pptx_1330405012.pdf)
