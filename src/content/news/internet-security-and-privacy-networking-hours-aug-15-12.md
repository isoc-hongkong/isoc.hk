**INTERNET SECURITY AND PRIVACY NETWORKING HOURS**  
The Internet has an enormous impact on our daily lives. It provides us with a great source of information and it changes the way people communicate. However, it bring about threats due to attacks and abuse of use. You are the first line of defense over these threats. Protect yourself now and reduce the risk of being a target. Don't miss the great opportunity to learn and implement some Internet security and privacy strategies! |  |
