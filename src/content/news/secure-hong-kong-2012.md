**SecureHongKong 2012**  
Internet Society Hong Kong Members Enjoy a Complimentary Seat and Earn up to 7.5 CPE credits! Don’t forget to enter your ISOCHK Discount Code (ISOCMB) during registration. **Limited Seats!** *FREE shuttle bus service will be provided before and after the conference on the event date based on a first-come, first-served basis (details to be advised).  ![Image - Register Link](/images/uploads/2013/01/Image-Register_Now_Green.jpg)   **RSVP by Dec 10, 2012** Jointly organized by _**(ISC)², Internet Society Hong Kong, Professional Information Security Association and Cloud Security Alliance - Hong Kong & Macau Chapter**_ and supported by _**Office of the Government Chief Information Officer**_ , the first _**SecureHongKong 2012**_ will be held on 17 Dec 2012 at Cyberport.  The one day conference will focus on how security professionals can address the constantly changing security landscape. In addition, it will provide the attendees with an opportunity to understand emerging technologies and how its adoption can help them mitigate the risk their organizations face with the increased sophistication of targeted attacks.
