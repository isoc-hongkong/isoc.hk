Scalability • DevOps • ProgLang**   
Entering its fifth year since 2012, this annual and signature Summit continues to put the spotlights on the latest development, news, technologies, advanced hints and tips.   
      * 2-day Summit will be packed with main track **Conference,** diverse track **Workshops** and**Seminars**
      * **An exhibition** will be concurrently run with local tech companies and enterprise demonstrating the latest tech-product and ideas.
      * Learn from renowned **speakers/leaders** and share of **insight/ideas** on the diverse development platform/environment. **Summit speakers and supporters in previous years of WIDS including Google, Dropbox, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, Microsoft, Mozilla, Adobe, IBM, Paypal, Alibaba and much more!**
      * Networking and matchmaking opportunities among**+500 attendees of hirable developers and hiring companies within!**
      * Evaluate**latest state of art technologies and products** in town!
